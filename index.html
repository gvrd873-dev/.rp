Guidelines

Server Rules

Breaking these rules may result in warnings, kicks, or bans. Read them carefully.

1 General Discord Rules:

  • Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
  • – Avoid repeated messages, excessive images, or blocks of text.
  • No NSFW content, illegal material, or graphic violence.
  • Do not promote other servers, personal projects, or services without staff permission.
  • All members must follow Discord rulesAll members must follow Discord rules at all times.
  • Staff have final authority in disputes or rule enforcement. Arguments with staff will not be tolerated.
  • Do not pretend to be another player, staff member, or public figure.

2 Peacetime Strict Alert

  • FRP Speed Limit: 70 mph
  • Crimes: No crimes allowed (examples: no car theft, no robberies, no assaults)
  • Behavior: Players must follow all laws and roleplay realistically. Any illegal action is considered fail roleplay.
  • EX-1: Cannot chase or attack other players without a scripted scenario.
  • EX-2: Must obey traffic signals and speed limits.

3 Peacetime Regular Alert

  • FRP Speed Limit: 80 mph
  • Crimes: Very minor crimes allowed (examples: minor traffic violations, jaywalking, small shoplifting)
  • Behavior: Players can bend rules slightly but must remain realistic.
  • EX-1: Speeding to avoid a minor accident is allowed.
  • EX-2: Small theft during roleplay may occur with consequences.

4 No Peacetime Alert

  • FRP Speed Limit: 95 mph
  • Crimes: Major crimes allowed (examples: bank robberies, high-speed chases, assaults in roleplay)
  • Behavior: Players have more freedom but must follow roleplay logic.
  • EX-1: Planning and performing a bank heist.
  • EX-2: 

5 Fail Roleplay (FRP):

  • Fail roleplay is any action that breaks realistic roleplay rules
  • Avoid Driving recklessly without reason
  • Avoid Ignoring roleplay events or other players’ actions
  • Avoid Interfering with ongoing roleplay without consent
  • Consequences: Warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.

6 Department Rule

  • You must be a Civilian before applying to any department.
  • Follow all department-specific rules and uniform requirements.
  • Inactivity without notice may result in removal from a department.
  • Do not abuse department tools or vehicles outside of sessions.
  • Represent your department with professionalism at all times.

8 Staff Section

  • Follow the chain of command at all times.
  • Do not argue with staff decisions publicly — use the ticket system.
  • Do not impersonate staff members.
  • Punishment appeals must be submitted via ticket only.
  • Staff have final say in all moderation decisions.

7 Roleplay Etiquette 

  • Wait your turn in roleplay scenarios.
  • Follow instructions from staff or scenario leaders.
  • Avoid interrupting or rushing events.
  • Help new players understand roleplay rules.

8 Vehicle Restrictions

  • Please remember, you can drive specific vehicles if you have @Rank 1 @Rank 2 @Rank 3 roles.
  • Driving without one of the vehicle exemption roles will lead to a strike.

9 Additional Rules:

  • No Exploiting or Cheating – Bugs, glitches, or hacks are forbidden.
  • Stay in Character – Avoid metagaming or OOC advantages.
  • Name & Appearance – Use realistic names and appearances. Offensive or inappropriate names are prohibited.
  • Vehicle & Property Rules – Respect property and server economy; stealing or destroying items without roleplay reason is forbidden.
  • Communication – Use clear and respectful communication. Avoid excessive OOC in roleplay channels.
  • Scenario Cooperation – Work with others and staff to ensure smooth roleplay events.
  • Report Bugs or Exploits – Report immediately; using exploits before reporting is punishable.
